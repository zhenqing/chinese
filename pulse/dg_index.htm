April 25 2010

YD Staffs Attended OMF Conference

On April 25, seven YD staff members attended Breakthrough Mission Conference held by Overseas Missionary Fellowship (OMF) in Bay Area Redwood City. OMF is formerly China Inland Mission and Overseas Missionary Fellowship. OMF serves the churches and bring gospel to some 12 countries in East Asia and have a pioneering ministry in others.
